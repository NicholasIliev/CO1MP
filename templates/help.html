How do I know if I've gotten an exercise right?

Once you have finished your exercise and want to know wether your answer is correct or not, you must click on the "submit" buttom - which is located just below the text editor.

Then if you have managed to get the correct answer, the message "You passed all tests!" shall be displayed on your screen.

However, if you get the answer wrong, meaning that there's a mistake in your code, the message "You failed on tests" shall appear on your screen.

How does the leaderboard work?

The leaderboard uses a point system to determine the ranking of a user.

Based on the difficluty of the exercises you complete, and the number of exercises you complete correctly - we take the total of points that you have earned and hence we determine your global ranking. Each correct exercise grants a user a certain number of points.

The point system works as follows:


  1. • An 'Easy' difficulty or a 'Green' coloured exercise is worth: 1 point

  2. • A 'Medium' difficulty or an 'Orange' coloured exercise is worth: 2 points

  3. • A 'Hard' difficulty or a 'Red' coloured exercise is worth: 3 points
